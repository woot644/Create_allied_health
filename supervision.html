Overview

Create Allied Health offers AASW-accredited clinical supervision for social workers at all stages of their career. Our supervision practice is grounded in reflective, strengths-based approaches that support professional growth, ethical practice, and clinical excellence. Whether you are a new graduate, an experienced practitioner seeking advanced development, or a social worker navigating complex ethical terrain, we provide a safe, structured space for professional reflection and learning.

Professional Development

Our supervision supports the development of advanced clinical skills across a range of practice areas.

  • Advanced assessment skills — Refining clinical assessment approaches including psychosocial assessment, risk assessment, and functional impact evaluation
  • Intervention techniques — Developing and strengthening evidence-based intervention strategies across diverse client populations
  • Case management — Building expertise in complex case coordination, systems navigation, and multi-agency collaboration

Ethical Guidance

Social work practice regularly involves navigating complex ethical dilemmas. Our supervision provides a structured framework for ethical decision-making.

  • Navigating ethical dilemmas — Working through complex scenarios using established ethical frameworks and the AASW Code of Ethics
  • Professional standards — Ensuring practice aligns with AASW practice standards and regulatory requirements
  • Legal requirements — Understanding and meeting mandatory reporting obligations, privacy legislation, and duty of care responsibilities

Reflective Practice

Reflective practice is the cornerstone of professional growth. Our supervision creates a safe, non-judgmental space for critical self-reflection.

  • Identify strengths — Recognising and building on your existing skills, knowledge, and practice wisdom
  • Areas for improvement — Honest, supportive exploration of areas where further development will enhance your practice
  • Strategy development — Creating actionable plans for professional growth and skill enhancement

Quality Assurance

Regular clinical supervision is essential for maintaining high standards of practice and ensuring positive outcomes for clients.

  • Maintaining high practice standards aligned with AASW requirements
  • Ensuring evidence-based approaches are applied consistently
  • Supporting clinical governance and accountability
  • Preventing burnout and promoting practitioner wellbeing

Practice Areas

Our supervisor has extensive experience across a wide range of social work practice areas, enabling supervision that is relevant and applicable to your specific context:

  • School-based therapy — Supporting social workers delivering therapeutic services in educational settings
  • Child protective services — Supervision for practitioners working in child protection and out-of-home care
  • Grief and bereavement counselling — Supporting practitioners working with loss, grief, and end-of-life
  • Hospital-based chronic illness — Supervision for social workers in acute and subacute health settings
