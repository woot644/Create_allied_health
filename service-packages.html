Professional Partnerships

Clinical Social Work
Subcontracting & Partnerships

We partner with insurers, hospitals, aged care providers, and case management organisations to deliver specialist clinical social work services. Our packages are designed for seamless integration into your existing workflows, with flexible engagement models and transparent billing.

01

iCare & WorkCover

Comprehensive clinical social work services for workers compensation and lifetime care clients.

Services Include

  • Psychosocial assessment
  • Discharge planning
  • Housing assessment
  • NCAT applications
  • Mental health support
  • Family mediation
  • Expert reports
  • Post-injury adjustment counselling
Rate: In accordance with AASW recommended rates
