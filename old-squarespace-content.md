## *Working together to create change!*

Create Allied Health Services is a Sydney-based, NDIS-registered provider offering social work led therapeutic supports across multiple sectors.

We work with:
- NDIS participants
- iCare clients
- Private hospitals
- Private clients
- Older adults transitioning to aged care
- Families navigating complex systems

Our services include trauma-informed support in disability, housing, mental health, post-hospital recovery, and life transitions. We provide practical advocacy, mediation, and system navigation — always with compassion and dignity at the core.

We also offer accommodation and housing support across SDA, SIL, public, private, and transitional pathways. Additionally, we provide high-quality clinical supervision for social workers, students, and allied health professionals.

**Need support navigating life's toughest systems?** We'll walk beside you.

### CATEGORY: Our Services

#### 1. Disability Support
At Create Allied Health Services, we provide trauma-informed, NDIS-registered social work and allied health support for people living with disability. We understand the unique health, housing, and life challenges many NDIS participants face — and we're here to help you navigate them with confidence.

Our services are personalised, compassionate, and focused on your individual goals. Whether you need help with system navigation, housing support, plan reviews, or psychosocial support, we'll walk beside you every step of the way.

#### 2. Rehabilitation
Whether you're recovering from a serious injury, neurological condition, workplace accident, or complex illness, our team is here to support your rehabilitation journey with compassion and clarity.

Create Allied Health Services provides **iCare-funded** and **insurance-supported** social work and psychosocial support for people navigating recovery, hospital discharge, and long-term rehab. Our person-centred approach helps you regain confidence, re-engage in daily life, and access the services you need to heal — physically, emotionally, and practically.

We collaborate with private hospitals, GPs, discharge planners, and rehab teams to ensure your transition back into the community is safe, supported, and sustainable.

#### 3. Mental Health
We provide trauma-informed mental health support and psychosocial services for NDIS participants, iCare clients, and individuals facing emotional distress or complex life challenges.

Our team works collaboratively with you to build personalised recovery plans that align with your goals, strengths, and daily needs. Whether you're managing anxiety, trauma, grief, or psychosocial disability, we help you access the tools, services, and supports to improve wellbeing and quality of life.

From recovery coaching to emotional regulation and system navigation, we're here to walk beside you — with empathy, respect, and real-world expertise.

#### 4. Transition Support
We provide compassionate, expert support during life's most complex transitions — including hospital discharge, prison release, NDIS to aged care, and major health or housing changes.

Our team works closely with iCare, NDIS planners, private hospitals, justice services, and support coordinators to ensure continuity of care and dignity through every step.

Whether you're returning home from rehab, exiting the justice system, or facing a sudden diagnosis, we'll help you navigate services, connect to housing, and regain control.
