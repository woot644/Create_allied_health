Service Areas

Greater Sydney

Face-to-Face Services Across Sydney

Create Allied Health Services provides in-person allied health support across Greater Sydney. Our AASW-registered clinical social workers deliver face-to-face services in homes, hospitals, community settings, and offices throughout the metropolitan area. Whether you need psychosocial assessments, hospital discharge planning, NDIS support, or complex case management, our team is available in your local area.

Hospital Partnerships

Sydney Hospitals We Work With

Our clinical social workers coordinate closely with hospital social work departments and treating teams across Sydney's major hospitals. We provide discharge planning, psychosocial assessments, and post-discharge support to ensure safe, coordinated transitions from hospital to home or community care.

We work with private and public hospitals across Greater Sydney for discharge planning, rehabilitation support, and care coordination. Our clinical social workers provide in-reach services and coordinate with hospital treating teams to ensure safe, timely transitions from hospital to home or community care.

Beyond Sydney

Telehealth — Regional NSW & National

Geography should never be a barrier to quality allied health support. Our secure video telehealth services bring the same experienced, AASW-registered clinical social workers to clients across regional New South Wales and every state and territory in Australia.

Regional NSW

Newcastle, Wollongong, Central Coast, Blue Mountains, Coffs Harbour, Port Macquarie, Wagga Wagga, Dubbo, Tamworth, Orange, Bathurst

National Coverage

All states and territories via secure video telehealth. Same clinical social workers, same quality of care, no geographic barrier. Available across Victoria, Queensland, South Australia, Western Australia, Tasmania, ACT, and the Northern Territory.

What We Offer

Our Services

Wherever you are, our full range of clinical social work and allied health services is available to you. Explore our specialist areas below.

Psychosocial Support

Comprehensive psychosocial assessments, reports, and therapeutic intervention for NDIS, iCare, and DVA clients.

Hospital Discharge Planning

Safe, coordinated transitions from hospital to home or community care, working alongside treating teams.

Housing & Accommodation

Supporting older Australians and their families through aged care assessments, placements, and transitions.

Mental Health Support

Trauma-informed mental health intervention, crisis support, and ongoing therapeutic care.

Access requests, plan reviews, support coordination, and specialist NDIS social work.

Clinical Supervision

AASW-accredited clinical supervision for social workers, counsellors, and allied health professionals.
